Kottayam

Kottayam is a marvelous coastal town of Kerala that is located at a distance of 60 km from Kochi. Not only is the city famous for the mesmerizing view of the backwaters, but it is a trading hub as well. Many tourists leave out Kottayam while planning there Kerala trip, but they do not know what they are missing.

The town is quite close to some of the most picturesque sight-seeing spots in India, including Aruvikkuzhi waterfalls, Elaveezha Poonchira, Kumarakom, and many more. This city also boasts of some of the most famous temples of Kerala, namely Vaikom temple, Bharananganam, and many more. Kottayam is also the home to the mesmerizing Vembanad lake that is famous all around the globe. If you visit during certain months of the upcoming year, you will be able to take part in exciting activities like viewing the boat race, relish delicious dishes in the annual food festival, and many more.

Kottayam, a town situated in south-central Kerala is an epitome of beauty between Kerala hill country and Vembanad. Popularly known as a hub of trade and commerce, the district is an eminent destination for literature and education. Situated amidst the beauty of nature, Kottayam has a lot to offer to its tourists. Offering breathtaking beauty with its majestic hills and scenic valleys, the district is largely visited by nature lovers. In addition to the surrounding beauty, Kottayam also offers a number of waterfalls that accentuates the beauty of the southern town. The huge spray of water droplets fills the surrounding air with extravagant scenic beauty and a positive vibe.

Tourist Attractions

Kottathavalam

A fascinating cave carved out of rock surface is Kottathavalam lying on the top of a Murugan Hills at Kurisumala. The cave is considered to be the resting place for Madura Royal family.

Nadukani

After all the blessings from temples, it is time for some adventure. Nadukani is one such place nestled amid the wilderness of nature and densely wooded hills offering its visitors a beautiful trail for trekking.

Illikal Kallu

Located at a height of 6000 ft above the sea level in the Western Ghats of Kottayam, Kerala, Illikkal Kallu is a major tourist attraction and one of the most pristine spots in all of India. Situated in Moonnilavu Village, Meenachil taluk, the nearest city to the summit is Teekoy.
